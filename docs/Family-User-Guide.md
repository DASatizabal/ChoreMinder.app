## üìã Managing Chores

### For Parents: Creating and Assigning Chores

#### Creating a One-Time Chore
1. **Navigate to Chores**
   - Click "Chores" in the main navigation
   - Click "Create New Chore"

2. **Fill Out Chore Details**
   - **Title**: Clear, specific name (e.g., "Clean your bedroom")
   - **Description**: Detailed instructions
   - **Category**: Select from cleaning, organizing, outdoor, etc.
   - **Points**: Reward value (10-50 points typically)
   - **Due Date**: When the chore should be completed
   - **Priority**: Low, Medium, or High
   - **Estimated Time**: How long it should take
   - **Photo Required**: Toggle if verification photo needed

3. **Assign to Family Member**
   - Select who should do the chore
   - Add any special notes
   - Click "Create Chore"

#### Creating Recurring Chores
1. **Set Up Recurrence**
   - When creating a chore, toggle "Recurring"
   - Choose pattern:
     - **Daily**: Every day, weekdays only, or custom days
     - **Weekly**: Specific days of the week
     - **Monthly**: Specific date each month
     - **Custom**: Advanced patterns

2. **Schedule Examples**
   - "Make bed" - Daily, every morning
   - "Take out trash" - Weekly, every Tuesday
   - "Clean room" - Weekly, every Saturday
   - "Wash car" - Monthly, first Saturday

### For Children: Completing Chores

#### Viewing Your Chores
1. **Dashboard Overview**
   - See today's chores at the top
   - View upcoming chores in "Next Up"
   - Check overdue chores (highlighted in red)

2. **Chore Details**
   - Click any chore to see full instructions
   - View points you'll earn
   - See due date and priority
   - Read any special notes from parents

#### Completing a Chore
1. **Start the Chore**
   - Click "Start" when you begin
   - Timer starts tracking your work

2. **Mark as Complete**
   - Click "Mark Complete" when finished
   - Add any notes about the work done

3. **Photo Verification (if required)**
   - Take a clear photo showing completed work
   - Upload the photo
   - Wait for parent approval

## üéÆ Gamification & Rewards

### Points System
- **Base Points**: Set by parents when creating chores
- **Bonus Points**: Earned for:
  - Completing early
  - Excellent quality work
  - Maintaining streaks
  - Participating in challenges

### Achievements
Unlock special badges for:
- **First Steps**: Complete your first chore
- **Streak Master**: Complete chores 7 days in a row
- **Speed Demon**: Complete 5 chores under time estimate
- **Team Player**: Help with family challenges

### Challenges
Join family or personal challenges:
- **Weekly Family Goal**: Complete 20 chores as a family
- **Speed Challenge**: Complete all daily chores before noon
- **Quality Challenge**: Get 5 chores approved in a row
- **Category Master**: Complete 10 chores in one category

### Redeeming Rewards
1. **Browse Rewards**
   - Go to the Rewards tab
   - See available rewards and point costs

2. **Request a Reward**
   - Click "Redeem" on desired reward

3. **Common Reward Types**
   - **Privileges**: Extra screen time, choose family movie
   - **Treats**: Special dessert, favorite snack
   - **Activities**: Friend sleepover, special outing
   - **Items**: New book, small toy (with budget limit)
   - **Experiences**: Stay up late, breakfast in bed

## üÜò Quick Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**"I can't see my chores"**
- Check if you're logged into the correct account
- Verify you've accepted the family invitation
- Try refreshing the page

**"Photo won't upload"**
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure the photo is under 5MB
- Try taking a new photo

**"Points aren't showing"**
- Points are awarded when chores are approved
- Check if the chore requires parent verification
- Look in your notification history

**"Notifications not working"**
- Check your notification preferences
- Verify your contact information is correct
- Check if you're in quiet hours
